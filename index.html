How to Use This App
PEZA Registered Devices — Concur Inventory Tracker
What is this app?
This is the PEZA Registered Devices Inventory Tracker for SAP Concur Philippines. It manages and monitors all IT equipment registered under the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), covering the full lifecycle of each device — from PEZA permit details to supplier, cost, condition, and current status.
Dashboard
  • The Dashboard tab shows a status breakdown of all devices as colored cards (e.g. Deployed, Disposed, Missing).
  • Click a status card to see how that status is distributed across the three device types (SAP, Concur Only, TUSA) in the summary panel below.
  • The summary panel will not change the inventory list immediately — click "View in Inventory" to apply the filter and switch to the Inventory tab.
  • Click a card a second time or press ✕ on the summary panel to deselect.
Device Types
Devices are classified into three types:
  • SAP-I4P-ISP — Active SAP-registered devices; with assigned equipment number.
  • Concur Only — Devices registered before SAP integration; no equipment number assigned.
  • TUSA — Devices acquired from Anthem Solutions, Inc. (ASI).
Inventory List
  • The Inventory tab shows all 16,000+ device records in a sortable, paginated table.
  • Use the search bar to find devices by serial number, description, asset number, permit number, supplier, and more.
  • Use the filter chips (Category, Type, ABC, Status) to narrow down the list. Active filters are highlighted in blue. Click a chip again or press × to clear it.
  • Click any column header to sort ascending or descending.
  • Click the View icon (📄) in the PEZA 8105 column to open the associated permit form PDF directly in the app.
  • Click any row to open the full device detail panel.
  • Use the Export CSV button to download the currently filtered list.
Device Detail & Editing
  • Clicking a row opens the detail modal, showing all 31 fields organized into 5 sections: Device Identity, PEZA Registration, Procurement, Financial, and Notes.
  • The hero strip at the top shows the status badge and key facts (category, type, floor, condition) at a glance.
  • Click Edit to modify any field. All 31 Excel columns are editable, including dropdowns for Status, Category, Type, Floor, and Condition.
  • Click Save to apply changes or Cancel to discard and return to the view.
  • Click Delete to remove a record — a confirmation prompt will appear before deletion.
Syncing & Importing Data
  • To sync the latest data from Excel, run node build_data.js in the project folder. This reads the master tracker and regenerates data.js.
  • Alternatively, use the ⬆ Import Excel button in the top-right header to load an .xlsx or .csv file directly in the browser — no build step required.
⚠ Note on TUSA Acquisition Cost
The acquisition cost shown for TUSA devices (e.g. ₱23,417,147.65) is a batch total covering all TUSA devices under that permit — it is not the cost per individual unit.
